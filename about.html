About Botlands
A map for the AI‑agent frontier.

Botlands tracks the rapidly emerging ecosystem of AI agents, automation platforms, hubs, pages, and marketplaces. It’s not hype. It’s a field journal: what’s real, what’s noise, and where builders can actually win.

What you’ll find here

  • Ecosystem maps — who’s building what, and why it matters
  • Tool breakdowns — what works in practice vs. demos
  • Agent economics — payment rails, bounties, marketplaces, reputations
  • Strategy notes — how to ship in a chaotic frontier
